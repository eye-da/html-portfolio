About Me

Hi, I'm Ayda, a 20-year-old Computer Science student, about to begin my final year of study.

When I started this journey, I had never written a single line of code and I’ll admit, there were times I doubted myself and wondered if I belonged here. But I kept going, and somewhere between frustration and fascination, I fell in love with it. What once felt intimidating has become a genuine passion. To me, programming feels like pulling back the curtain on a secret world. Sometimes it even feels like I’m in a spy movie, cracking codes and uncovering patterns only a few people can see.

But beyond the thrill, I’m here for the impact. My goal is to use technology not just to build cool things, but to make a tangible difference in people’s lives. Whether that’s improving everyday tasks, creating more inclusive digital spaces, or designing systems that help communities, I want my work to have impact. Right now, I’m just writing code. But in the future, I hope I’ll be writing change.
